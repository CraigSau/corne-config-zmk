Quick Reference

Bluetooth Controls:

  • Lower + Ctrl = Clear all connections & enter pairing mode
  • Lower + A/S/D/F/G = Switch to bluetooth profile 0/1/2/3/4

Common Programming Symbols:

  • Brackets: Raise + A=<, S=>, D=[, F=], J={, K=}
  • Operators: Raise + G=_, H=-, J==, N=+
  • Special: Raise + C=\, V=|, X=~, M=", comma=?
  • Optimized: Underscore on left hand for easier access

Navigation:

  • Arrow Keys: Lower + H=Left, J=Down, K=Up, L=Right
  • Numbers: Lower + top row (Q=1, W=2, etc.)

Display Info:

  • Shows current layer, battery %, and WPM
  • Auto-sleeps after 30 seconds idle
  • Deep sleep after 5 minutes
  • Bluetooth TX power boosted for Windows compatibility
